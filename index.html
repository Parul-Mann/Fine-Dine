Welcome to FineDine



FineDine, a multi-cuisine restaurant that offers a unique dining experience with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. Our restaurant is dedicated to serving high-quality dishes that are carefully crafted by our experienced chefs. From Indian and Italian to Chinese and Mexican, we offer a wide range of cuisines that are sure to satisfy all taste buds.

At FineDine, we believe in using fresh, local ingredients that are sourced from nearby farms and suppliers. Our commitment to sustainability and the environment is reflected in our menu and business practices. We strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where our guests can relax and enjoy their meals in style.

Thank you for choosing FineDine for your dining experience. We look forward to serving you soon.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Locally sourced ingredients

FineDine focuses on using locally sourced ingredients, which ensures that the food is fresh and of high quality. This commitment to using local produce supports the local economy and promotes sustainability.

02

Multi-cuisine options

FineDine offers a diverse range of cuisines, including Indian, Italian, Chinese, and Mexican, providing guests with a variety of options to choose from. This ensures that everyone can find something they love on the menu.

03

High-quality dining experience

FineDine provides a high-quality dining experience with an inviting atmosphere, excellent service, and delicious food. The restaurant is perfect for special occasions, date nights, or just a casual meal with friends and family.

Seasonal Specials, freshly made for you!

Shrimp Coconut Curry with Rice

Shrimp, Coconut Milk, Rice, Spices

Fresh plump shrimp simmered in a fragrant curry sauce of coconut milk, spices, and aromatics, served over fluffy rice cooked with coconut milk and lemongrass. A creamy, savory, and aromatic dish perfect if you are craving a hearty dish with some freshly caught seafood.

Citrus Blast Fruit Salad

Mixed Citrus Fruits, Blueberries, Nuts, Seeds, Honey, Mint Leaves

A refreshing mix of citrus fruits, including juicy oranges, tangy grapefruits, and zesty lemons, tossed with a drizzle of honey and topped with fresh mint leaves. A burst of bright, sweet, and tangy flavors that awaken the senses and invigorate the palate. Perfect for a light and healthy dessert or a bright and colorful side dish.

Spicy Ramyun with fresh Cod

Ramyun Noodles, Fresh Cod, Chili Paste, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Green Onions

A spicy and flavorful dish featuring tender chunks of fresh cod and savory Ramyun noodles in a broth made with garlic, soy sauce, and fiery chili paste. Topped with sliced green onions for a fresh burst of flavor, this dish packs a punch and is sure to satisfy any craving for bold and spicy flavors. Perfect for a warming and satisfying meal on a chilly day.

Christmas Special Pita and Hummus

Pita Bread, Chickpeas, Tahini, Garlic, Lemon Juice

A festive and delicious dish featuring warm, fluffy pita bread served with a creamy and flavorful hummus made with smooth chickpeas, tahini, and tangy lemon juice. The hummus is delicately spiced with garlic and other seasonings to create a rich and satisfying dip that perfectly complements the warm and pillowy pita bread. A delightful appetizer or side dish that is sure to please any crowd during the holiday season.

Green Godess Spinach Fruit Salad

Spinach, Mixed Fruits, Avocado, Green Onions, Lemon Juice

A fresh and healthy salad featuring tender spinach leaves, juicy mixed fruits, and creamy avocado slices. Topped with sliced green onions and a zesty dressing made with tangy lemon juice, this salad is a burst of bright and refreshing flavors. The perfect balance of sweet and savory, this salad is both satisfying and nourishing. A great option for a light and energizing lunch or as a colorful side dish for any occasion.
